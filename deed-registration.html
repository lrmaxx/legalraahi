Overview of Gift Deed Services

A Gift Deed is a legally binding document representing the voluntary transfer of a gift from one person to another. At LegalRaahi, we provide comprehensive Gift Deed services to ensure that your gift transfers comply with all legal provisions. Our services cover both movable and immovable properties, allowing you to express your affection by gifting valuable assets to your loved ones.

Steps for Drafting a Gift Deed with LegalRaahi

  • Step 1: Begin the deed by including essential details such as the date and place of execution, donor and donee information, property details, and the signatures of the parties and witnesses.
  • Step 2: LegalRaahi helps you procure the required stamp paper based on your state's regulations.
  • Step 3: We assist you in getting the Gift Deed registered at the appropriate registrar or sub-registrar office.

Documents Required for Gift Deed Registrations

  • Original Gift Deed
  • ID proofs (e.g., Driver's License, Passport)
  • PAN Card
  • Aadhar Card
  • Documents establishing the donor's title to the property
  • Any other related agreements or certificates
