Overview of Immigration

Immigration is the process of moving to another country for various reasons, including work, study, investment, or personal pursuits. It involves complying with a set of legal procedures, visa applications, and fulfilling specific criteria laid out by the destination country. Immigration opens doors to global opportunities, diverse experiences, and a chance to start a new chapter in life.
