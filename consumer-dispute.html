Consumer Dispute Resolution

Consumer disputes can arise from a variety of issues, including unfair contracts, defective products, and deficient services. We understand that the legal jargon and complex procedures can be overwhelming, but we're here to guide you through every step of the process.

  • Use Plain Paper: You can file your complaint on plain paper. There's no need for stamp paper for the declaration.
  • Provide Essential Details: Ensure your complaint includes all the necessary details about both the complainant and the opposite party.
  • Choose Your Filing Method: You have options for how to register your complaint. It can be done in person, directly by the complainant, or through an authorized agent. Alternatively, you can send it by post, addressed to the Redressal Agency.
  • Nominal Fees: The fees charged for filing are quite reasonable and depend on the value of the claim.
